Privacy Policy for Reno.owl

Last updated: May 2026

Privacy Policy for Cyrio Tech Pte Ltd

At Reno.owl, accessible at https://cyriotech.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information collected and recorded by Reno.owl and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@cyriotech.com.

This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application and online services. By using Reno.owl, you hereby consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Information We Collect

When you register for a Reno.owl account, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name and address
  • Profile information relevant to your role (firm owner, project manager, or client)

When you use the app, we also collect:

  • Project information, updates, and progress data
  • Photos and media files uploaded to projects, snagging reports, and portfolios
  • Messages and communications within the platform
  • Calendar events and scheduling information
  • Documents uploaded to the platform
  • Device information and push notification tokens for sending app notifications

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Reno.owl mobile application
  • Enable communication between interior design firms and their clients
  • Send push notifications about project updates, snagging issues, and announcements
  • Improve and personalise your experience within the app
  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

Data Storage and Third Party Services

Reno.owl is built on Google Firebase infrastructure. Your data is stored and processed using the following Google services:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages secure user login
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your project, client, and firm data
  • Firebase Storage — stores uploaded photos, documents, and media files
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — delivers push notifications to your device

Google's privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Your data is stored on Google Cloud servers in the Asia Southeast region (Singapore).

Data Access and Sharing

Reno.owl operates on a strict multi-tenant architecture:

  • Firm owners and project managers can access data belonging to their own company only
  • Clients can only access data related to their own project
  • No user can access data belonging to another firm
  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes

Push Notifications

Reno.owl sends push notifications to keep you updated on project progress, snagging issues, and announcements. You can manage notification preferences through your device settings at any time.

Account Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your Reno.owl account and all associated personal data. To request account deletion, please contact us at info@cyriotech.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Reno.owl is a professional platform designed exclusively for the renovation and interior design industry, intended for use by business owners, project managers, and their clients in a professional capacity. As such, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at info@cyriotech.com and we will promptly remove such information from our records.

GDPR Data Protection Rights

Every user is entitled to the following rights:

  • Right to access — You may request copies of your personal data
  • Right to rectification — You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — You may request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions
  • Right to restrict processing — You may request that we limit how we process your data
  • Right to data portability — You may request transfer of your data to another organisation or directly to you
  • Right to object — You may object to our processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@cyriotech.com. We will respond within 30 days.

PDPA (Singapore Personal Data Protection Act)

As a Singapore-incorporated company, Cyrio Tech Pte Ltd complies with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA). We collect, use, and disclose personal data only for purposes that a reasonable person would consider appropriate in the circumstances.

For PDPA-related enquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at info@cyriotech.com.
